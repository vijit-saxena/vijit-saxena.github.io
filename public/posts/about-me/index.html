About Me

February 25, 2026 · Vijit Saxena

Life is a lot like a distributed backend, multiple tasks to perform each day, multiple roles to fit into, but interestingly all by a single individual. At least this is not the case when working on a software application where I can define exact responsibilities and expectations from each service running individually.

Hi, I am Vijit, currently developing distributed reliable systems for the fintech industry here in India. It is fascinating to design how a distributed application can communicate amongst itself and perform a certain task. As fascinating as it could get while designing each component of a distributed system, it gets more and more interesting building one by hand, and the fascination does not stop there, it grows seeing pods spin up in real time, scale, perform and not to mention break.

All the blogs here would be written by me, and not AI, talking about not limited to tech-related stuff but spanning daily experiences as a software developer, managing a team, design discussions and some system failures I witnessed. It will be my utmost priority to write here with a human touch and not in a very professional language, mentioning my experiences.

Now it would be a lie to say there is no selfish motive for me sitting behind a computer, typing in dim yellow light late at night. I do have one. I would like to document what goes on around me and within me so I can keep track of my learnings, and I believe typing things out might have a longer impact on my senses when making any decisions in the future. Although it wouldn’t be a lie to say there are no monetary benefits I seek from here. I guess my work takes care of that part for now.

I would also encourage keeping your views to yourself and disagreeing or even strongly disagreeing about a few things here, but that’s fine.

Who gives a FUCK!
